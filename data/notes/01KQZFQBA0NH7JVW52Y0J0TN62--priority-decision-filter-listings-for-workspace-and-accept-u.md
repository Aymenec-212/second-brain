I decided to prioritize reliable Wi‑Fi and a dedicated workspace for the next trip. Practically, that means filtering listings for the word 'workspace' and at least one recent review explicitly mentioning 'fast Wi‑Fi' or 'good for working'. If the average Airbnb in Lisbon was €90/night, I'm willing to accept up to €120/night (+€30/night). For a 4‑night trip that raises accommodation from €360 to ≈€480. I also committed to buying an eSIM before departure (≈€15), packing/buying a spare USB‑C (~€15) and a 20,000 mAh power bank (€45) — combined gear ≈€75. Given these numbers, I rechecked the budget and set an upper cap of €1,000 for a 4‑night city break that includes work conveniences.
