title: Session paces for marathon-specific training (MP, tempo, intervals, easy)
type: fact
---

Target training paces established for the plan: Marathon pace (MP): 5:20 per kilometer (matching 3:45 marathon target). Tempo/threshold work: generally 5:00–5:10 per kilometer; this should include some sustained threshold efforts and marathon-pace segments. Interval/VO2 work (track or quality repeats): target paces around 3:40–3:50 per kilometer for 1 km reps; early progressions may use 800m reps at ~3:50–4:00/km. Easy/recovery runs: 5:50–6:20 per kilometer. Long runs are progressive: start easy and include occasional finishes where the last 10–12 km are at marathon pace. These paces guide interval sets, tempo distances, and targeted long-run segments; they will be adjusted slightly if tune-up race results or ongoing training indicate a need to shift intensities.
