Recruiter said “around €92k.” I am treating that as an annual gross base figure for now because no equity, bonus, or total-comp details were mentioned. My current gross is €78k. At my current startup I could try to negotiate up to about €85k gross, but there’s no committed promotion or formal path promised there. Financially I estimate a move to €92k would be roughly a +€14k (≈18%) increase — about €1,166 gross/month extra, translating to ~€850–900 net/month depending on taxes/social. My personal target to justify a move is at least €95–100k gross, or a clear equity/promotion roadmap that effectively makes the offer equivalent in long-term upside. Until I confirm bonus/equity ranges, the quoted €92k is insufficient on its own to meet my threshold.
