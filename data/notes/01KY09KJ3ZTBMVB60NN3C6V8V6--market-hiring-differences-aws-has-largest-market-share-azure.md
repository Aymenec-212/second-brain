title: 'Market & hiring differences: AWS has largest market share; Azure strong in
  enterprises'
type: fact
---

AWS holds the largest market share and therefore the broadest global job market and third‑party ecosystem; this tends to result in more hiring opportunities across startups, cloud-native companies, and consultancies. Azure is particularly strong inside enterprises that already use Microsoft products such as Windows Server, Active Directory, Microsoft 365 (Office 365), Dynamics, and SQL Server; those organizations often prefer Azure because of licensing ties, single-vendor support, and tighter integrations. In short: if I want maximum hiring flexibility and exposure to a wide variety of cloud-native services and startups, AWS increases my odds. If I target corporate IT shops, managed enterprise roles, or organizations invested in Microsoft technology stacks, Azure is often the better bet. This affects resume decisions, which certifications to pursue initially, and the types of hands‑on projects I should build to demonstrate relevant skills.
