Positive outcomes I want to keep: a walking-heavy plan worked well — my phone recorded about 48 km total (average ~10–12 km/day), which kept the days active and flexible. The Tram 28 experience was worth doing once despite queues. Belém pastries (Pastéis de Belém) were memorable at roughly €3 each. The best meal of the trip was dinner at Taberna da Rua das Flores on 2026-04-29 — cost ≈ €46 per person including wine; worth prebooking for repeat visits. MAAT on 2026-04-30 was visually excellent and easy to handle because I prebooked an €11 entry slot online. Staying in a compact base in Alfama paid off: it allowed short returns to the apartment between walks and made the day feel less rushed.
