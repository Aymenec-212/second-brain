title: 'Certifications & career path: comparable cert structures; choose based on
  target employers'
type: fact

Both AWS and Azure offer clear certification paths that are recognized by employers. AWS commonly positions certifications as: Cloud Practitioner (fundamental) → Solutions Architect / Developer / SysOps → Specialty certs (security, networking, ML, etc.). Azure offers a similar progression: Fundamentals (AZ‑900) → Administrator / Developer / Solutions Architect → role‑based and specialty certifications. Which certs to pursue should be driven by the jobs I want: AWS certs are widely recognized across startups and cloud-native companies; Azure certs carry strong weight for enterprise Microsoft shops. Certifications are useful signals but are not a substitute for hands‑on experience; combining one or two relevant certifications with demonstrable projects or lab work is the most effective approach for hiring managers.
