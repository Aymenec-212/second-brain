Compensation and budget plan for the pilot and next steps: during the pilot keep current on-call pay at €200/week. Add a quality-of-life comp rule: if an on-call week exceeds 3 pages, that on-call receives 1 paid compensatory day off. Short-term pay increase cap: the team can agree to a pay increase up to +€150/week per person if justified by pilot results and budget constraints. Hiring plan: target to request budget approval for hiring an SRE by 2026-07-15 with earliest possible hire start in 2026-09. Stopgap option: contract an external database incident provider for critical nights at approximately €1,000/month; include a short cost-benefit analysis in the proposal to Marion comparing external provider vs. increased on-call pay vs. hiring. Use pilot KPIs to build the hiring case—if the pilot demonstrates measurable QoL improvements with maintained reliability, propose €100–150/week increase for permanent on-call or expedite SRE hire depending on budget.
