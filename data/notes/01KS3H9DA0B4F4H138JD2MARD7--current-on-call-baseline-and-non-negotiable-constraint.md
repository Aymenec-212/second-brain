Baseline: I am a 31-year-old backend/ML engineer at a Paris logistics startup with a five-engineer team (Sam, Nora, Marc, Leïla, Jonas). Current on-call rotation is 7-day rotations where each engineer is on call about every five weeks. On-call window is 18:00–09:00 weekdays plus full weekends. When I am on call I average roughly three pages per week. Current on-call pay is €200/week per person. Hard constraint: family time is non-negotiable — I must be present for dinner with my partner Emma (relationship 2 years). Practical operational goals I am setting: maximum pages per night should be 2, mean time-to-acknowledge (MTA) target 15 minutes, mean time-to-repair (MTTR) target 90 minutes, and weekends maximum 4 total pages. Timeline constraints: CTO Marion wants a proposal by 2026-06-15; target to pilot changes before summer. Hiring SRE is a medium-term goal with budget approval targeted by 2026-07-15 and earliest start date for hire 2026-09. These baseline facts and constraints will be the foundation for proposals, pilots, KPIs, and escalation rules.
