I resolved to prebook one co‑working day as a backup because I had to do about four hours of work on 2026-04-30 and didn't want to gamble on finding a seat in cafés. Estimated cost for a day pass is ~€12 (examples: CoworkLisbon or HedenLabs). If I don't use the booked day, I will cancel it or simply skip attending. This eliminates the risk of being forced to work from the bed or a noisy café during a short trip.
