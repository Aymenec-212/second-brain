Monetization mechanics locked for launch: offer a 7-day free trial for new premium signups. Billing will be processed via the Apple App Store and Google Play Store in-app subscription systems. Subscriptions are per account (not per-device) to accommodate logistics teams sharing a single account. Free users retain live maps and basic routing; premium unlocks offline map downloads, advanced routing (v2), and GPX export. Initial customer acquisition spend will be minimal — rely on in-app prompts and email onboarding to convert free users to trial and then paid. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) and broader marketing spend remain TBD and will be tracked as part of post-launch metrics.
