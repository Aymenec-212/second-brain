Decision: do an independent Sintra day (not a guided tour) to move at my own pace and take photos. Practical transit and timing plan: leave the Alfama Airbnb at 07:00, reach Rossio station by ~08:00 (allowing ~30–40 minutes for tram/metro/walk), take the ~08:20 train from Rossio to Sintra (approx. 40 minutes), arriving ~09:00. From Sintra station, use the local bus or tuk-tuk/shuttle to climb to Pena Palace. Because Pena Palace gets crowded, buy a timed-entry ticket for Pena (target 10:00 entry) to avoid long queues and to secure a morning window for photos. Plan to visit Quinta da Regaleira in the afternoon; buy that ticket in advance if online availability exists. Cost estimates to budget into the Sintra day: Pena ~€14–20, Regaleira ~€10–12, roundtrip train ~€5–6, plus lunch and minor extras — set aside €70 for the day. Rationale: independent day is cheaper and gives flexibility to linger at viewpoints, take photographs, and avoid being locked into a fixed guided itinerary.
