title: Thursday tempo/threshold sessions — template and progression
type: idea
---

Thursday tempo session template and progression: begin with 8–10 km of threshold/tempo work at approximately 4:50–5:10 per kilometer. As training progresses, extend tempo work to 12–16 km total with the option to include structured marathon-pace segments (for example, 2×8 km with the final 4 km of each at 5:20/km, or a continuous 12–16 km at threshold). The Thursday session focuses on sustained efforts that raise lactate threshold and marathon-specific endurance. Incorporate variability within the session (steady tempo, tempo with MP finish, or longer tempo with marathon-pace blocks) depending on the block and proximity to tune-up races. Keep total session volume including warm-up/cool-down within the 10–14 km evening workout window so it fits post-work schedule. If recovery is poor, reduce tempo length or substitute a threshold-in-interval format.
