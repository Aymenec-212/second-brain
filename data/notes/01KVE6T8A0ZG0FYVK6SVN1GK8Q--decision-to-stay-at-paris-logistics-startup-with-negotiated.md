Decision (2026-06-18): remain at my current job as a 31-year-old backend/ML engineer at a Paris logistics startup. I negotiated a +10% salary increase and a trial of a 4-day workweek starting in September. Primary reasons for staying: the +10% keeps my savings plan on track, and the 4-day week is the experiment I need to improve life balance. Known risks: the startup’s inherent instability, potentially slower visible promotion trajectory compared with moving companies, and uncertainty about whether the 4-day week trial will actually deliver a genuine day off rather than hidden expectations or uncompensated work. Working hypothesis / plan: accept the raise immediately, run a formal 3-month 4-day-week trial (Sept–Nov 2026) to test whether it yields the intended life-balance improvements without unacceptable productivity loss, then review outcomes in December 2026. If after the trial the productivity and compensation trajectory aren’t acceptable, I will revisit moving to another company. Immediate requirement: obtain written confirmation of the agreed terms (salary and trial) from management/HR.
