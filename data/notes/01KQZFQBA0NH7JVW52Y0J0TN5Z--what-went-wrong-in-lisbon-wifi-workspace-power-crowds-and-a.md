  attempt'

Problems and frictions to address next time: the Airbnb Wi‑Fi was flaky with 2–3 dropouts per day, and the apartment did not have a dedicated desk, so I had to work one afternoon from the bed. I forgot my USB‑C charger and bought a replacement for €25. I also didn't bring a power bank and had the phone die twice; I bought a 20,000 mAh power bank for €45. Tourist crowds around Rossio felt overpriced for food and packed, and Tram 28 had long queues mid‑day (best to go early). There was a minor pickpocket attempt near Praça do Comércio — no material loss, but significant stress. These are concrete pain points to eliminate or mitigate on the next short work/leisure trip.
