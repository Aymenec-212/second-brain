title: Priorities and dealbreakers for considering a move
type: decision
---

My priority ordering for any job move is explicit and non-negotiable: 1) a faster path to Staff/Principal engineering within 12–18 months; 2) work that is ML-heavy (substantial ML projects rather than primarily backend/infra); 3) salary (I want at least €95–100k gross or an offer that includes clear equity/promotion upside to justify a lower base). Dealbreakers: if the role is mainly backend engineering with little ML focus, or if there is no clarity or credible roadmap for promotion to Staff/Principal, I will not proceed. Compensation alone is third priority — I am willing to consider trade-offs if the role accelerates my move to Staff and contains meaningful ML work and equity potential. I will use these criteria as a filter during the initial call.
