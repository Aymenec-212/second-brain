Packing checklist (carry-on only; 40 L target): essential clothing for three days (light layers, one nicer outfit for fado dinner), comfortable walking shoes, light rain jacket, underwear/socks, quick-dry T-shirts, one pair of pants, sleepwear. Electronics and work kit: laptop (able to do brief remote work), charger, phone and cable, small power bank, headphones, universal adapter. Travel documents and money: passport/ID, printed/phone copies of flight and Airbnb, cards (primary card = work card for bookings), small cash pocket ~€80 for markets and vendors. Toiletries in travel sizes, any meds, and a compact umbrella. Operational notes: check airline carry-on size/weight limits before packing; keep valuables in the carry-on; plan outfits to avoid checked baggage; prepare a small daypack for Sintra with water and snacks. Also add a note to message the Airbnb host about early check-in possibility for the May 1 evening arrival to avoid long check-in queues.
