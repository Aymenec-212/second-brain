type: idea

Tuesday hard session template and progression: initial phase sessions start with 6×800 m repeats targeted at roughly 3:50–4:00/km effort, with 2–3 minute easy jog recoveries between repeats. Warm-up and cool-down included; total session volume expected to be 8–12 km including warm-up/cool-down. Over the months this will progress toward 6–8×1 km repeats at 3:40–3:50/km with similar recovery; sets and volume will increase gradually while monitoring fatigue. The Tuesday workout is the primary VO2/leg-speed stimulus and should be prioritized if a session must be modified for time or soreness. If niggles or fatigue appear, reduce reps or shorten recovery, and in persistent issues swap a run for cross-training rather than forcing intervals. Record specific rep paces, reps, and perceived exertion each week to track progression and readiness.
