Finalized product/monetization stance for the near-term roadmap: Cairn remains freemium. Premium tier priced at 4.99 EUR per month at launch and will include offline maps plus advanced routing and GPX export. No launch discounts; maintain the 4.99 EUR/mo price at initial public release. Billing will be handled through the mobile platform stores (Apple App Store and Google Play) as in-app subscriptions. The premium offering will be per-account (not per-device) so logistics teams can share accounts under a single subscription. This price and bundle are locked for launch decision-making, marketing copy, and subscription configuration.
