Budget and hiring plan summary: initial development runway assumes one internal FTE (existing headcount) plus one external contractor for three months. Contractor spend estimate for the three sprints is €15k–€25k depending on rate; total initial external spend for the three-month push is estimated at €30k–€45k when accounting for additional external costs and contingencies. Post-launch retention option: a contractor retainer of approximately €2k/month for up to six months to cover urgent native maintenance until a full-time mobile hire is recruited. Hiring timeline: plan to hire a dedicated mobile engineer by Q1 2027 to take ownership of native code, maintenance, and further mobile features. These figures are initial estimates for planning; track actual contractor rates and hiring timelines in the finance and recruiting trackers.
