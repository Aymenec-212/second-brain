title: Conversion targets, revenue scenarios, and validation metrics
type: decision

Conversion targets and revenue scenarios to design for: primary product goal is 5% conversion among users who download an offline pack (design funnels and UX for 5%). Use a conservative baseline of 2% for financial modelling and investor/ops forecasts. Example revenue calculations: with 1,000 MAU and 5% conversion => 50 subs => 50 * 4.99 EUR = 249.50 EUR/month (~2,994 EUR/year). With 10,000 MAU and 5% conversion => 500 subs => 2,495 EUR/month (~29,940 EUR/year). These are conservative baseline scenarios to validate unit economics before scaling. Single success metric at 3 months post-launch: 5% conversion among users who downloaded an offline pack within 90 days of public launch. Secondary numerical target: 500 paying users by 2026-12-31 if MAU scales. If the 5% conversion is met within 3 months, consider offline premium validated and iterate toward routing and wider-region packs.
