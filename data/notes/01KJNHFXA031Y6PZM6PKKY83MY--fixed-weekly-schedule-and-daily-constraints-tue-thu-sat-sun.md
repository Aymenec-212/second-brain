Committed weekly layout: four runs fixed to specific days — Tuesday intervals (evening after work), Thursday tempo/threshold (evening after work), Saturday long run (morning), Sunday easy recovery run 8–10 km or full rest depending on fatigue. Strength work: two 20-minute sessions per week on Monday and Friday mornings focusing on posterior chain and glutes. Full rest days from running are Wednesday plus Monday/Friday outside the strength sessions (strength is brief and scheduled as maintenance). Evening work commitments make weekday runs preferable on Tuesday and Thursday. Long runs on Saturdays can be up to 2.5–3 hours on peak weeks. This schedule is firm: Tue/Thu/Sat/Sun runs, Mon/Fri strength, Wed rest. The plan accepts switching Sunday to rest occasionally if the Saturday long run is particularly hard, but otherwise keeps Sunday as a short recovery run.
