Decided on a 5‑gel schedule for the 3‑hour run, with the first gel at 0:00 and then one every 40 minutes: 0:00, 0:40, 1:20, 2:00, 2:40. Assume each gel ≈100 kcal, so 5 gels = ~500 kcal from gels. Add a small banana at ~90 minutes for ~90 kcal, giving a total of ~590 kcal during the run. Caffeine limit: cap total caffeine to ≤200 mg. To stay within that, use caffeinated gels only twice: the gels at 1:20 (80 minutes) and 2:00 (120 minutes) will be caffeinated; the gels at 0:00, 0:40 and 2:40 will be non‑caffeinated. This schedule balances continuous carbohydrate intake (one gel every 40 minutes) with a mid‑run whole food (banana) and keeps caffeine intake conservative to reduce the risk of GI symptoms or excess stimulation. Note: I haven’t recently tested five gels in 3 hours; treat this as the plan but be ready to modify if GI issues occur.
